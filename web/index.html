Fourierify

Sketch a gesture, then watch epicycles choreograph it.

Draw a fluid line anywhere on the canvas. When you lift the pointer, a constellation of rotating circles will redraw it using Fourier magic. Use the slider to decide how many orbiting circles keep the dance precise or boldly abstract.

Hold to draw → release to compute epicycles. Slider adjusts the number of circles in motion.
